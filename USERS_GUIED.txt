==== GEMS HIDDEN ====

START - exit the title the screen and pause the game
B - run
A - pick up the purple shiny glitter stuff
Dpad - move in that direction

HINT: your follower will point you in the direction of a real glitter if you stop moving.
HINT: there is fake glitter, be aware of your partner's direction.
